Topic 1 вҖ” Direction Cosines & Direction Ratios
HOT в–ј

Direction Cosines (DC) kya hote hain?

Agar ek line x, y, z axes ke saath angles Оұ, ОІ, Оі banati hai, to un angles ke cosines ko direction cosines kehte hain вҖ” denoted by l, m, n.

Direction Cosines Definition
l = cos Оұ  |  m = cos ОІ  |  n = cos Оі

рҹ“Ң Key Identity: lВІ + mВІ + nВІ = 1 (ALWAYS)

Direction Ratios (DR)

DC ke proportional values jinka naam direction ratios hai вҖ” denoted by a, b, c.

DC from DR
l = a/вҲҡ(aВІ+bВІ+cВІ)
m = b/вҲҡ(aВІ+bВІ+cВІ)
n = c/вҲҡ(aВІ+bВІ+cВІ)

Line joining two points P(xвӮҒ,yвӮҒ,zвӮҒ) and Q(xвӮӮ,yвӮӮ,zвӮӮ)

DR of line PQ
a = xвӮӮвҲ’xвӮҒ  |  b = yвӮӮвҲ’yвӮҒ  |  c = zвӮӮвҲ’zвӮҒ

Distance PQ = вҲҡ[(xвӮӮвҲ’xвӮҒ)ВІ + (yвӮӮвҲ’yвӮҒ)ВІ + (zвӮӮвҲ’zвӮҒ)ВІ]
вҡЎ Shortcut Trick вҖ” Yaad Karo

lВІ+mВІ+nВІ=1 вҶ’ Ye identity har question mein use hoti hai. Agar options mein l,m,n diye hain to pehle check karo kya sum of squares = 1 hai, galat option eliminate ho jayega!

рҹ“Ң Important Points
  • DC of x-axis = (1, 0, 0); y-axis = (0, 1, 0); z-axis = (0, 0, 1)
  • Agar line ka sense reverse ho to DC negative ho jate hain
  • DR unique nahi hote, DC unique hote hain (upto sign)
  • Ek line ke do sets of DC hote hain: (l,m,n) aur (вҲ’l,вҲ’m,вҲ’n)

Angle Between Two Lines

cos Оё = |bвӮҒвғ— В· bвӮӮвғ—| / (|bвӮҒвғ—| Г— |bвӮӮвғ—|)

Parallel Lines Condition
aвӮҒ/aвӮӮ = bвӮҒ/bвӮӮ = cвӮҒ/cвӮӮ (same DR ratios)

вҡЎ Parallel vs Perpendicular вҖ” Easy Check

Perpendicular вҶ’ Dot product = 0 (multiply corresponding DRs aur add karo = 0)

Topic 3 вҖ” Skew Lines & Shortest Distance
IMPORTANT в–ј

Skew Lines kya hoti hain?

Wo lines jo na parallel hain, na intersecting вҖ” ye 3D mein hi possible hai. Inke beech ka shortest distance = Common Perpendicular ki length.

SD between rвғ—=aвӮҒвғ—+О»bвӮҒвғ— and rвғ—=aвӮӮвғ—+ОјbвӮӮвғ—
SD = |(aвӮӮвғ—вҲ’aвӮҒвғ—)В·(bвӮҒвғ—Г—bвӮӮвғ—)| / |bвӮҒвғ—Г—bвӮӮвғ—|

Cartesian Form SD
SD = |det([xвӮӮвҲ’xвӮҒ, yвӮӮвҲ’yвӮҒ, zвӮӮвҲ’zвӮҒ; aвӮҒ,bвӮҒ,cвӮҒ; aвӮӮ,bвӮӮ,cвӮӮ])| / вҲҡ[(bвӮҒcвӮӮвҲ’bвӮӮcвӮҒ)ВІ+(cвӮҒaвӮӮвҲ’cвӮӮaвӮҒ)ВІ+(aвӮҒbвӮӮвҲ’aвӮӮbвӮҒ)ВІ]
рҹ“Ң Lines Coplanar hone ki condition
  • SD = 0 hona chahiye
  • Determinant |xвӮӮвҲ’xвӮҒ, yвӮӮвҲ’yвӮҒ, zвӮӮвҲ’zвӮҒ; aвӮҒ,bвӮҒ,cвӮҒ; aвӮӮ,bвӮӮ,cвӮӮ| = 0
  • Agar do lines coplanar hain to ya to parallel hain ya intersect karti hain
вҡЎ SD Formula Trick вҖ” 3Г—3 Determinant

Numerator = 3Г—3 det banao: Row 1: (aвӮӮвҲ’aвӮҒ, bвӮӮвҲ’bвӮҒ, cвӮӮвҲ’cвӮҒ) | Row 2: (aвӮҒ,bвӮҒ,cвӮҒ) | Row 3: (aвӮӮ,bвӮӮ,cвӮӮ). Denominator = |bвӮҒГ—bвӮӮ| = cross product ka magnitude.

Topic 4 вҖ” Equation of a Plane
HOT в–ј

Vector Form

Normal nвғ— wala plane, point aвғ— se guzarta hai
rвғ— В· nМӮ = d  вҶ’  rвғ— В· nвғ— = aвғ— В· nвғ—

General equation of plane

Through (xвӮҒ,yвӮҒ,zвӮҒ) with normal (a,b,c)
a(xвҲ’xвӮҒ) + b(yвҲ’yвӮҒ) + c(zвҲ’zвӮҒ) = 0

Plane Through Three Points

Points A(xвӮҒ,yвӮҒ,zвӮҒ), B(xвӮӮ,yвӮӮ,zвӮӮ), C(xвӮғ,yвӮғ,zвӮғ)

|xвӮӮвҲ’xвӮҒ yвӮӮвҲ’yвӮҒ zвӮӮвҲ’zвӮҒ| = 0
|xвӮғвҲ’xвӮҒ yвӮғвҲ’yвӮҒ zвӮғвҲ’zвӮҒ|

Angle Between Two Planes

Planes: aвӮҒx+bвӮҒy+cвӮҒz=dвӮҒ and aвӮӮx+bвӮӮy+cвӮӮz=dвӮӮ

Perpendicular: aвӮҒaвӮӮ+bвӮҒbвӮӮ+cвӮҒcвӮӮ = 0
Parallel: aвӮҒ/aвӮӮ = bвӮҒ/bвӮӮ = cвӮҒ/cвӮӮ
N В· N = cos Оё
Normal vectors ka dot product = cos of angle between planes

Topic 5 вҖ” Distance from Point to Plane
IMPORTANT в–ј
Distance from P(xвӮҒ,yвӮҒ,zвӮҒ) to plane ax+by+cz+d=0
d = |axвӮҒ + byвӮҒ + czвӮҒ + d| / вҲҡ(aВІ + bВІ + cВІ)

D = |dвӮҒ вҲ’ dвӮӮ| / вҲҡ(aВІ + bВІ + cВІ)

P(xвӮҒ,yвӮҒ,zвӮҒ) se ax+by+cz=d par foot Q

Point ko equation mein substitute karo, absolute value lo, niche wala вҲҡ(aВІ+bВІ+cВІ) = normal vector ki magnitude. Same structure as 2D distance from point to line!
